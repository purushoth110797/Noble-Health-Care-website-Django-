Our Pricing and Insurance Policy

Transparent Pricing

We believe in clarity when it comes to healthcare costs. Our pricing is competitive and reflective of the expertise of our medical professionals and the state-of-the-art facilities we offer. You can find detailed information about the cost of our services, enabling you to make informed decisions about your healthcare.

Insurance Coverage

We understand the importance of insurance in managing healthcare expenses. We work with a wide range of insurance providers to make our services accessible to as many individuals as possible. Our team is here to assist you in verifying your insurance coverage, processing claims, and addressing any insurance-related queries you may have.

Financial Assistance

For those without insurance or facing financial challenges, we offer flexible payment options and financial assistance programs. Our goal is to ensure that everyone in our community can access the care they need without undue financial burden.

Billing Transparency

Our billing process is designed to be transparent and straightforward. You will receive detailed invoices that clearly outline the services provided. If you have any questions about your bill, our team is available to assist you and provide the necessary clarification.

Flexible Payment Options

We understand that everyone's financial situation is unique. That's why we offer flexible payment plans to accommodate your individual needs. Our team will work with you to find a payment solution that suits your budget.

Assistance Programs

We are committed to supporting our community. Our financial assistance programs are designed to help individuals and families facing economic hardships. Please reach out to our financial assistance team to discuss eligibility and the application process.

Peace of Mind

Your health is our priority, and we want to ensure that financial considerations do not hinder your access to quality healthcare. By providing transparent pricing, working with various insurance providers, and offering financial assistance, we aim to give you peace of mind during your healthcare journey.

For any billing or insurance-related inquiries, please contact our dedicated team at NHC@customercare.com.
